## ✨ Features

### 💌 Guest Experience
* **Envelope Intro:** A cinematic opening animation to set the tone (Toggleable).
* **Interactive RSVP:** Easy-to-use form for guest attendance and preferences.
* **Music Player:** Integrated audio controls for a curated wedding playlist.
* **Photo Gallery:** High-resolution display of engagement and couple photos.
* **Live Countdown:** Real-time ticker counting down the seconds to the "I Do's."
* **Story Timeline:** A dedicated section for the couple's journey (Toggleable).

### 🔐 Management & Admin
* **Central Dashboard:** Full overview of guest responses and site activity.
* **Activity Logging:** Real-time tracking of site interactions and RSVP updates.
* **Modular Control:** Enable or disable major sections (Envelope, Comments, Story) directly from the code.
* **Comment Section:** Digital guestbook for well-wishes (Toggleable).

## 🛠️ Customization

This project is designed to be modular. You can enable or disable specific features within `page.tsx` to fit your needs.

### Enabling Modules
Open `src/app/page.tsx` and look for the feature flags to toggle the following:
* **Envelope Intro** (Intro animation)
* **Comment Section** (Guestbook)
* **Story Page** (Couple's history)
